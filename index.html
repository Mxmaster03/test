About

C_Studio03 is a visual practice rooted in elegance, emotion, and restraint. Every frame is shaped with a gallery mindset, where light, texture, and silence are treated as part of the composition.

From portraiture to wedding narratives and editorial stories, the work is guided by a refined minimalism that allows people, atmosphere, and memory to take center stage.
