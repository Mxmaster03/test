About The Artist

Charlton

I have always been more drawn to film than photography, to the way a moving frame breathes, the way a scene builds tension through light and silence. The still image, for me, is a single frame stolen from a film that exists only in my mind. I shoot the streets, the wild, and whatever catches my eye, not to record moments, but to compose them, the way a director would.

Based in Singapore
Shooting for passion, not commission
